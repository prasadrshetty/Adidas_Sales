1. Project Title
   Adidas Sales Performance & Insights Dashboard

2. Short Description
   This Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive view of Adidas sales performance by analyzing key business metrics such as revenue, profit, and units sold. It       helps uncover trends across time, regions, and product categories while identifying top-performing cities and retailers. The goal is to support data-driven
   decision-making by highlighting patterns, customer preferences, and effective sales strategies.

3.🛠️ Tech Stack
  Power BI – Data visualization & dashboard creation
  Microsoft Excel – Data source & preprocessing
  DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – Calculated measures & KPIs
  Power Query – Data cleaning & transformation

4.🔍 Techniques Used
  Data Cleaning & Transformation (Power Query)
  Data Modeling (Relationships between tables)
  KPI Cards for performance tracking
  Drill-down analysis (Year → Quarter → Month → Day)
  Interactive Filters & Slicers
  Custom DAX Measures for business insights

5.📂 Data Source
  Dataset sourced from Kaggle (Adidas US Sales Dataset)

6.✨ Features & Highlights
  📈 Overall Performance Metrics: Displays Total Sales, Profit, Units Sold, and Average Price
  🏬 Retailer Profit Analysis: Identifies top-performing retailers
  📅 Sales Trends Over Time: Tracks performance across different time periods
  👟 Product Category Insights: Highlights best-selling product categories
  👥 Customer Preferences: Analyzes sales by gender and product type
  🌐 Regional Analysis: Breaks down sales by region, state, and city
  🏙️ Top Cities by Profit: Identifies high-performing cities
  🛒 Sales Method Comparison: Evaluates In-store, Online, and Outlet performance
